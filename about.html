About

Joanne Rowling, born on 31 July 1965 in Gloucestershire, England, is the world-famous author of the Harry Potter series.

Career Highlights

She wrote 7 Harry Potter books between 1997 and 2007, selling over 600 million copies worldwide. She also writes crime fiction under the pseudonym Robert Galbraith.

Achievements

Rowling has received numerous awards, founded charities like Lumos, and inspired millions of readers globally.

Fun Fact: The Harry Potter series has been translated into more than 80 languages and adapted into blockbuster films.
