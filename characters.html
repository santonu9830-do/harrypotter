Harry Potter

Harry Potter is a beloved fantasy series created by J.K. Rowling that follows the journey of a young wizard destined to face dark forces. Set in the magical world of Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry, the story blends friendship, courage, and the struggle between good and evil.... ... Harry, along with his loyal friends Hermione Granger and Ron Weasley, learns spells, uncovers secrets, and battles challenges that test their bravery. The series explores themes of love, sacrifice, and identity, making it resonate with readers of all ages. Its mix of magic, mystery, and adventure has made it timeless worldwide.

Ron Weasley

Ronald Bilius Weasley is one of the central characters in the Harry Potter series — loyal, brave, and often the heart of comic relief, but also a key hero in the fight against Voldemort. .

Albus Dumbledore

Albus Percival Wulfric Brian Dumbledore is a fictional character in the Harry Potter series of novels by J. K. Rowling. For most of the series, he is the headmaster of the wizarding school Hogwarts. He is one of the most powerful and influential wizards of his time, and is the founder and leader of the Order of the Phoenix, an organisation dedicated to fighting the Dark wizard Lord Voldemort..
