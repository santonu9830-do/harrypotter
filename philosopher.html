✨ Harry Potter and the Philosopher's Stone ✨

Plot Overview

Harry Potter, an orphan living with his cruel aunt and uncle, discovers on his eleventh birthday that he is a wizard. He attends Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry, where he makes lifelong friends and uncovers the mystery of the Philosopher’s Stone. With the help of Ron Weasley and Hermione Granger, Harry faces challenges that test his courage and loyalty, ultimately confronting Lord Voldemort for the first time.

Key Characters

  • Harry Potter – The Boy Who Lived, discovering his magical heritage.
  • Hermione Granger – Intelligent and brave, always ready to help her friends.
  • Ron Weasley – Loyal and humorous, Harry’s first true friend.
  • Albus Dumbledore – The wise headmaster of Hogwarts.
  • Severus Snape – Mysterious Potions Master with hidden motives.
  • Lord Voldemort – The dark wizard seeking immortality through the Philosopher’s Stone.

Themes

Friendship, Courage, and The Power of Choice are central themes in this magical beginning.

Important Facts

  • Published: 1997
  • Series Position: Book 1
  • Major Magical Elements: The Philosopher’s Stone, Hogwarts, Quidditch, and the Mirror of Erised

Fun Fact: This book introduced readers to the wizarding world and the famous Hogwarts Express departing from Platform 9¾.
