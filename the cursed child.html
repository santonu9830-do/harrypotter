✨ Harry Potter and the Cursed Child ✨

Plot Overview

Years after the wizarding war, Harry Potter is now an overworked Ministry employee and a father. His son, Albus Severus Potter, struggles to live up to his father's legacy. Along with his friend Scorpius Malfoy, Albus discovers a Time-Turner and attempts to change the past. Their actions create dangerous alternate timelines, forcing Harry and his friends to fix history before it is permanently damaged.

Key Characters

  • Harry Potter – Now a Ministry worker and father facing new challenges.
  • Albus Severus Potter – Harry’s son struggling with identity and expectations.
  • Scorpius Malfoy – Draco’s son and Albus’s loyal best friend.
  • Hermione Granger – Minister for Magic in the future timeline.
  • Ron Weasley – Runs a joke shop and supports his friends.
  • Delphi – A mysterious girl with dark secrets.

Themes

Parent & Child Relationships, Friendship, and Consequences of Changing Time are central themes.

Important Facts

  • Published: 2016
  • Series Position: Story set after Book 7 (not a novel, but a play script)
  • Major Magical Elements: Time-Turner, alternate timelines, dark magic, legacy of Voldemort

Fun Fact: Unlike the original books, this story is written as a stage play script and was first performed in London.
